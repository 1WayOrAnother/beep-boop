Rules:

Beep Boop will count up to your number from 0 with the following exceptions:

  1. Numbers that contain a 1 are replaced with "Boop!
  2. Numbers that contain a 0 are replaced with "Beep!"
  3. Program translates numbers divisible by 3 into "I'm sorry, Fellow. I'm afraid I can't do that."
